Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 11, 2026

TeleDrive is a Flutter Android application that allows users to use their Telegram account as a private cloud drive. The app helps users upload, organize, preview, and download files using Telegram Saved Messages and private Telegram channels.

This Privacy Policy explains what information TeleDrive uses, why it is needed, and how user data is handled. By using TeleDrive, you agree to this Privacy Policy.

1. Information TeleDrive Uses

TeleDrive may ask for information required to connect your Telegram account and provide the app features. This may include:

  • Your Telegram API ID and API Hash, if required for app configuration.
  • Your phone number for Telegram authentication.
  • The verification code sent by Telegram during login.
  • Your two-factor authentication password, if 2FA is enabled on your Telegram account.
  • Files and media that you choose to upload, preview, download, or organize through the app.
  • Basic local app data needed to keep your session and settings working.

2. How TeleDrive Uses Information

TeleDrive uses this information only to provide the app functionality. This includes logging in to Telegram, showing your Saved Messages and eligible private channels, uploading files, downloading files, previewing files, organizing folders, and keeping your session active.

3. Telegram Account and Storage

TeleDrive uses Telegram as the storage system. Your files are stored in your own Telegram account, such as Saved Messages or private channels created or used by you. TeleDrive does not operate an independent cloud storage server for your files.

Your use of Telegram is also subject to Telegram's own terms and privacy policy.

4. Local Storage on Your Device

TeleDrive may store session information and app settings locally on your device to keep you logged in and make the app easier to use. The app uses secure local storage where appropriate.

5. File Access

TeleDrive accesses only the files that you choose to upload, download, or preview inside the app. The app does not sell your files, and it does not use your files for advertising.

6. Data Sharing

TeleDrive does not sell your personal information. TeleDrive does not share your personal files with third parties. Data is exchanged with Telegram only when needed for the app to work, for example during authentication, upload, download, folder management, and file preview.

7. Permissions

TeleDrive may request permissions needed for file selection, file saving, media preview, or other app features. These permissions are used only for the functionality requested by the user.

8. Security

TeleDrive is designed to use secure Telegram authentication and secure local session handling. However, no application can guarantee complete security. You are responsible for keeping your Telegram account, device, and login credentials safe.

9. Children's Privacy

TeleDrive is not designed for children under the age required by applicable law to use Telegram or similar online services. If you believe a child has provided personal information through the app, please contact us.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. When it is updated, the date at the top of this page will be changed. Continued use of the app after changes means you accept the updated policy.
